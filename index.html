How It Works

Five steps, source to publication.

From file upload to social-ready content, the tool stays close to the original text at every stage.

01
Step 1 · Upload

Upload a Source

Open the upload form and pick a file. An article, book chapter, sermon, or PDF. After submission, the text is automatically split, indexed, and stored in its own namespace. Larger sources take 1 to 3 minutes.

Open Upload Form →
02
Step 2 · Generate

Generate Text

Open the content generator, pick the uploaded source, and choose an output type: Q&A, article, key quotes, or social posts. Add specific topics or audience notes if helpful.

Open Generator →
03
Step 3 · Review

Review the Output

The finished text appears in reading mode with Copy HTML and Copy Text buttons. Before publishing, scan Scripture references and tone. The AI is set to quote verbatim and stay close to the source.

04
Step 4 · Graphic

Make a Graphic

Open the graphic generator, paste a verse or pull-quote into the main field, and pick a format. Adjust font, colors, and background. Export as PNG, JPG, or copy to clipboard.

Graphic generator coming soon
05
Step 5 · Publish

Use it in Comms

Drop the text and graphic into a newsletter, blog, social post, or print material. Everything is written in DMP's voice: faithful to Scripture, doctrinally precise, gospel-centered.

How it works

Four principles, built in.

01

Dedicated knowledge base

Every uploaded source lives in its own namespace. No mixing with other partners' material.

02

Faithful to the source

The AI is instructed to quote verbatim, preserve terminology, and never add applications the source itself doesn't teach.

03

Four output types

Q&A · Short article · Key quotes · Social media posts. Each tuned for a specific channel and reading level.

04

Reformed, gospel-centered voice

Generates in Chap Bettis's pastoral voice with Scripture references in standard format.
